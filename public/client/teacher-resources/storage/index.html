Storage

A service originally designed for our program participants is now available to all university students and the general public. Our storage space is heated, safe, and secure!

Storage options and prices

Bins

  • One 35-gallon bin – $8/month
    Add as many bins as you’d like for just $4/bin each month.

Bins are secured with complimentary tamper-proof seals to ensure that what is returned to you is exactly what you packed.

Empty bins may be picked up at our office location, 12 College Ave (Next to The Cocoa Bean) between 1-5pm. When they are full and ready to store, just bring them back to our office.

Too much stuff to fit in a bin? Go for the larger closet/stall option!

Closets

  • a. One 7 foot high 3′ x 3′ closet – $12/month
  • b. One 7 foot high 5’ x 5’ closet – $18/month
  • c. One 7 foot high 10’ x 5’ closet – $25/month

Closets will have a latch where customers can place their own lock in addition to the complimentary security seal.

We guarantee these are the lowest prices you’ll find in Rexburg. If you find a better deal, let us know and we’ll beat it by 10%!
